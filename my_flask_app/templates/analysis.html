Inside the Port Harcourt Rental Market

I scraped real rental listings across Rivers State, cleaned the inconsistencies, and broke the data down to understand how the market actually works. This isn’t just analysis. These are patterns developers, agents, and renters can act on immediately.

Market Expansion

Follow the Money: Where Developers Are Building

I compared property condition across different locations to see where new developments are actually happening. The pattern was clear. Growth is not evenly spread.

Business Finding:

New builds are heavily concentrated in Eliozu, Rumuodara, and GRA. If you are looking for high demand and fast appreciation, Eliozu stands out right now. Meanwhile, several other areas are dominated by older properties with little to no development activity.

Consumer Strategy

The Price of “Brand New”

I analyzed how property condition affects rent across similar locations. The gap between new and used properties is not small. It is a pricing strategy.

Business Finding:

“Newly built” properties come with a heavy premium. Renters who want to save money should target fairly used or recently renovated apartments. Once a property is no longer brand new, the rent drops noticeably, even in top areas.

Valuation Baseline

The GRA Effect

I broke down rent distribution by location to understand how much location alone influences pricing.

Business Finding:

GRA and Eagle Island operate in a completely different market tier. With rents pushing ₦6M on average, and going as high as ₦20M, they sit far above areas like Choba. Location isn’t just important here. It defines the entire price range.

Data Engineering

Cleaning the Market Before Analyzing It

Before any analysis, I audited the dataset to check for extreme values and inconsistencies that could distort the results.

Business Finding:

The dataset was heavily skewed by a ₦1.3 Billion commercial listing. After removing outliers and filtering out non-residential properties, the real rental market became clear. Most listings fall between ₦1M and ₦5M. Without this step, every insight would have been misleading.

Algorithm Prep

What Actually Drives Rent?

I ran a correlation analysis to identify which features truly impact price before building the model.

Business Finding:

The number of bedrooms strongly influences rent. Property size (square meters) showed no relationship with price because the data is unreliable. Many listings simply estimate it. I removed it from the model to avoid feeding it noise.
